Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This privacy policy describes how mywellnesscalc.com ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

Information we collect

Calculator inputs: All calculations performed on this site (BMI, calories, water intake, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the values you enter into our calculators. Your data stays on your device.

Analytics data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This may collect anonymised data such as pages visited, time on page, browser type, and approximate geographic location. This data cannot identify you personally.

Advertising: We display ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ad Settings.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies primarily for analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google AdSense). These are third-party cookies and are governed by Google's privacy policy. By using our site, you consent to cookies as described here. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some functionality may be affected.
